Chapter 2

The expedition will leave in a week, in which time Ava should start showing the new recruits around and starting their basic training. It seems Clemence is also in need of some clothes since his are in tatters and he doesn't have any spare with him.

Selene notices that the size of the Guild is way bigger than the amount of people walking around would need. Clemence starts filling in his name and information. She sees his full name is "Clemence Dukus" from the far colonies, and his previous occupation was miner.

Ava starts compiling a list of things needed for the journey. Explorers' handbook, training equipment, specialty lists. It would probably be best to ask Master Orlof, responsible for the archives, or Master Armatalla, head of the administrative branch. She takes Clemence and Selene to the administrative branch to introduce them to Master Armatalla as well. She allows me to make a list of the supplies we need, and she hands the rookies small pouches with advance pay for their work.

After that Ava takes them to Master Orlof, a very old man, with small round glasses perched on his nose. He gives the rookies each a book called "The Explorer's Guide to Exploring".

There is one more master, Master Roudaver, head of engineering, but he is not very social and would not appreciate random introductions.

Outside the Autumn Palace, Veronica and Dorian meet again. Veronica goes on about squeezing(?), and apparently she's homeless. Dorian mentions he is going to The Inbetween and invites her to come. They discuss the assignment Veronica got from his aunt. They realise they have very different views on life and knowledge.

They take the shortest route towards Dorian's office, and he gets the feeling they were being shadowed by several figures who are closing in on them. They dash into an alley but the figures dash after them and they start to gain fast. Veronica takes out a welding torch and burns the hinges off a door to a dingy hovel. They head up the stairs to another exit. Dorian tries to push a wardrobe in the way, but it doesn't budge. They are making good ground and manage to outrun the people following them as they burst out into a street with Greycap presence. To experience the feeling for travel, Veronica decides they should visit Aluminum Bay and Dorian follows.

Since they have a week before the journey, Ava takes Selene and Clemence to The Outpost to get them some drinks. They talk to the proprietor, Engine, who warns the rookies not to get killed. He says handling artefacts can be dangerous and points to a man with a half-molten face who had come into contact with the Blight. Ava tells them of her previous expedition and her encounters with the Blight. How it usually shows up as a red glow on flora. They talk to some of the other adventurers in the bar and make introductions. Clemence seems to immediately have lost the money he got before because his clothes are filled with holes, so they buy him some cheap workers clothes.

Ava, Selene, and Clemence made their way to the edge of Aluminum Bay, to see the Greatship they will be taking on their expedition. The outside is mostly red with with thick golden chains welded on, the red paint going into blue at the back of the ship in a gradient to symbolise a sunset. As Ava is giving a lecture about the workings of the Greatship, Dorian and Veronica scoot up and start listening and asking questions. Dorian mentions he is an archeologist and is going on a pilgrimage. Ava says she can ask Master Moska if they are in need of an archeologist and an artist. They seem to be convinced they are also leaving in a week so we decide to inquire at the Navigators Guild.

The group gets approached by a woman in uniform, Captain Atsæmæz, (she is the second captain not the captain albay) who would be on the Greatship, and thanks Ava for the work she's done on the hull of the ship. The whole group introduces themselves and the Captain says she looks forward to speaking with us more on the journey.

They discuss the Golgayad family, and Dorian mentions he knows of and admires Ava's grandfather Dacaro Aquilar. Selene mentions that she would like to learn more about her own family, and Dorian knows that her family is associated with the disappearance of a Greatship, the Hamurabi. The first officer of the Hamurabi expedition was called Dara, who was found adrift in an escape pod after the ship was lost. Dorian promised to do research on her family while she is training for the next week.

At the Navigators Guild we find a list with the manifest for the journey, which does list Dorian Golgayad and Veronica Buttons.

During the week, Veronica collects some (fine) tools from her patron for her work. Dorian looks for books on the Dara name and family. He also attends the last mass before leaving to appease his family. Ava spends the week training Selene and Clemence in their physicality, to up their endurance for the expedition, The Zalos.

At the setting-off day of the voyage, The Zalos is moored at The Maw. All are checked at the entrance and given a key to a cabin. Dorian is in a small room like this as well. Veronica has a very posh room with an entertainment system, her luggage already brought in, and a note welcoming her, and an itinerary attached to it.
